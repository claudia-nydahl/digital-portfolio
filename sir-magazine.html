This magazine is based on the famous fashion designer, Paul Smith. The name of the magazine is ‘Sir’, as the designer is known (Sir Paul Smith). The magazine is based solely on Paul Smith’s unique style ('classic with a twist'), his designs and his life. It shows the diversity, sophistication and creativity of his work as a designer. The magazine would target Paul Smith fanatics, stylish creatives and trend aware people. The style of the magazine is ‘classic with a twist’, mimicking the general style of Paul Smith’s designs. This is achieved through the use of a simple, geometric layout with bold and colourful elements.
