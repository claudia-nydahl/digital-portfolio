This magazine focuses on visual communication. This particular issue previews a feature of Japanese artist, Yayoi Kusama, who is known for her dot paintings and installation artworks which she draws from her hallucinations. She lives in a mental institution close to her art studio where she works every day. The magazine has a simple and clean design with bold colour. The magazine was made to be interactive with buttons, animation, sound and video.
