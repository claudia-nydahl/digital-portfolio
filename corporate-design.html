This corporate identity was created for a fictional sushi bar called Umi, meaning ‘sea’ in Japanese. The Japanese word for Umi is also a beautiful symbol, which is incorporated in the logo. Umi targets people that are looking for a fine dining, luxurious experience and traditional style Japanese sushi made by renowned Japanese chefs. The logo for Umi is a decorative and elegant Japanese style illustration of waves, placed in a circle that represents a sushi plate. The Japanese letter for Umi is also placed in the logo. A simple water droplet pattern was created to form part of the corporate identity. The colours chosen are a simple and elegant palette consisting of a deep blue with light grey and white. The font used throughout all designs is Gotham, for its clean, elegant and corporate look. This corporate identity is made up of the logo, two business cards, a letterhead and menu. Illustrations are incorporated in the menu to visually portray each classic sushi type.
