About Me

I am a graphic and web designer, having studied print publishing & design and web publishing & interactive media at Friends of Design. I am an art and design enthusiast and find delight in anything creative and quirky. I enjoy working with minimalism and a geometric style and have a particular taste for illustration and patterns. I believe in the importance of good design and the impact that it can have in communicating a strong and meaningful message.
