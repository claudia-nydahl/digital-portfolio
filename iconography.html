These icons represent 6 different Fashion Boutiques in Cape Town, on the theme of tourism. The icons consist of rounded squares to represent folded shirts and the patterned collar on each shirt portrays the style of a particular boutique store.

The Peter Pan collar with a polka dot pattern portrays the feminine and trendy clothing style of Me Me Me Boutique on Long Street. The Straight French collar with a simple, abstract pattern represents the elegant clothing style of Kat Van Duinen Boutique at the Old Biscuit Mill. The Jabot collar with a lace pattern evokes the vintage clothing style of Babette Boutique on Church Street. The Pointed Shirt collar with a geometric pattern depicts the classic with contemporary twist style of Mungo & Jemima Boutique on Long Street. The Double Shirt collar with a creative circular pattern shows the quirky, retro clothing style of A-List Boutique on Kloof and Long Streets. The Spread French collar and tie with a simple diagonal striped pattern portrays the sophisticated style of Blue Collar White Collar Boutique for men on Kloof Street.
